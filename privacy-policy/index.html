Privacy Policy

App: Zen Match

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Overview

Zen Match is a casual tile matching puzzle game. This privacy policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

Zen Match may store game progress, settings, coins, level progress, daily challenge progress, and collection progress locally on your device.

Advertising

Zen Match may use Google AdMob to show optional rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may provide in-game rewards such as coins or extra chances. Google AdMob may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, diagnostics, and ad interaction data according to Google's policies.

Crash and Diagnostics

Zen Match may use Firebase Crashlytics or another crash reporting provider to help detect crashes and improve app stability. Crash reports may include device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and diagnostic information.

Accounts and Backend

The current soft launch version of Zen Match does not require a user account and does not provide server-validated global leaderboards. Game progress is primarily stored locally on the device.

Real-Money Rewards

Zen Match does not offer real-money rewards, cash prizes, gambling, or betting features.

Children

Zen Match is not intentionally directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, contact us at: zenmatch.support@gmail.com
